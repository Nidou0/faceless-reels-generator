# Privacy Policy — Faceless Reels Generator

Last updated: 2026-06-28

Faceless Reels Generator ("the App") is a personal content-creation tool used
to generate and publish short-form video content to the operator's own
TikTok account.

## What data we access

The App requests the minimum TikTok API scope required to publish video
content directly to the authorized account (`video.publish`). The App does
not read, store, or process any other TikTok user's data, follower lists,
direct messages, comments, or analytics beyond what is necessary to confirm
a successful post.

## What data we store

The App stores, locally on the operator's own device:

- OAuth access and refresh tokens issued by TikTok, used solely to
  authenticate API requests made by the operator to their own account
- The video files generated by the App prior to upload

No data is transmitted to or stored on any third-party server. No data is
sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

## Data retention and deletion

Tokens and video files are retained locally until the operator deletes them.
The operator can revoke API access at any time via TikTok's account
settings, which immediately invalidates all stored tokens.
