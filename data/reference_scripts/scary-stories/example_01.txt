I found the door to my basement open again last night. I live alone. I always lock it. The lock was untouched, the wood wasn't forced, but it stood open six inches, exactly like it did three nights ago. I told myself it was the house settling. Old houses do that. But settling doesn't leave footprints in the dust that lead toward the stairs, not away from them. I went down with my phone flashlight, hands shaking more than I wanted to admit. Nothing was there. No broken window, no sign of forced entry, nothing out of place except one thing. On the bottom step, someone had written my name in the dust. Not scratched. Written, like with a finger. I haven't slept down there in years. So who was kneeling on my basement floor, close enough to write that?
